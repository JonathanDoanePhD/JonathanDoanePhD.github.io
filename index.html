Exploring the Neurological Frontier

In this groundbreaking project, we are exploring a new frontier in education by using advanced technology to understand how students learn!

We are studying a special type of data called "brainwave activity" to gain insights into their cognitive processes. By analyzing the brainwave patterns of students who experienced confusion while learning, we aim to develop a better understanding of their comprehension levels.

We are utilizing cutting-edge tools like TensorFlow Keras to build a computer model that can decode these intricate brainwave patterns. This innovative approach has the potential to redefine the way teachers assess student understanding and personalize their instruction. Through this project, we are at the forefront of exploring the fascinating world of neuroscience and its application in education.
