Accurate Speech Analysis

Have you ever struggled with the inefficiency of traditional audio analysis methods? The standard approach, based on brief pauses and volume dips, fails to account for the unique vocal qualities of each individual.

That's why I developed a cutting-edge audio analyzer that accurately counts words in speech, outperforming traditional methods by 200%. This was achieved through a combination of multiple linear regression techniques and a deep understanding of vocal qualities.

With this innovative solution, I am proud to have made a meaningful contribution to the field of communication. This project was completed within one week at the Erdős Institute, showcasing my dedication and drive to create impactful technology.
